Submission Guidelines

Please review the following information carefully before starting the form submission process.

General Information
  • Full Name: The full name of the victim
  • Guardian's Name: The name of the guardian
  • Gender: Select the gender (Male, Female, Other)
  • Age: The age of the victim
  • Address: The full residential address of the victim
  • Police Station & District: Provide accurate police station and district details
  • Contact Number: A valid phone number following (+88)01XXXXXXXXX
  • National ID/Birth Certificate: Provide a valid identification document
  • Email Address: Ensure a valid email for further communication
  • Participation Role: Specify the role during the movement
Incident Details
  • Incident Type: Choose the type (Injured, Missing, Death)
  • Incident Date: The exact date of the incident
  • Incident Location: Location where the incident occurred
  • Incident Description: Provide a detailed description (min 10 chars, max 500 chars)
Contact Information of Related Person
  • Contact Person Name: Full name of the contact person
  • Relationship to Victim: Specify the relationship
  • Contact Person's Phone: Valid phone number of the contact person
  • Contact Person's Address: Address of the contact person
Supporting Documents
  • Injured Person's Photo: Upload a clear JPG/PNG photo (Max 1MB)
  • Medical Report (PDF): Upload a scanned PDF report (Max 5MB)
  • Death Certificate: Upload the death certificate for death cases (PDF)
  • Police Report: Upload a police report for missing cases (optional but recommended)
Email Verification

Please ensure to verify your email before proceeding with the submission. A verification link will be sent to your email address upon initiating the process.
